# komoChat by Qbexel Website Structure and Content Plan

## 1. Sitemap

*   **Home Page (`/`)**: Introduction to komoChat by Qbexel, key benefits, and a strong call to action.
*   **Features Page (`/features`)**: Detailed explanation of the AI agent's capabilities.
*   **About Us Page (`/about`)**: Story of komoChat by Qbexel, mission, vision, and team (if applicable).
*   **Contact Us Page (`/contact`)**: Ways to get in touch, inquiry form.
*   **Privacy Policy (`/privacy`)**: Standard privacy policy page.
*   **Terms of Service (`/terms`)**: Standard terms of service page.

## 2. Content Plan by Page

### Home Page (`/`)

*   **Hero Section:**
    *   **Headline:** 


"komoChat by Qbexel: Humanized AI for Social Media Business Automation"
    *   **Sub-headline:** "Seamlessly integrate AI to handle messages, queries, and sales 24/7, balancing human and AI synergy."
    *   **Call to Action (CTA):** "Get Started" or "Request a Demo"
*   **Introduction to komoChat by Qbexel:** Briefly explain what it is and its core purpose.
*   **Key Benefits Section:**
    *   24/7 Sales & Support
    *   Automated Message Handling (images, voice, chat)
    *   Product Query to Order Fulfillment
    *   Human-AI Synergy
*   **How It Works (Brief Overview):** Simple, high-level steps.
*   **Testimonials/Social Proof:** Short quotes from hypothetical satisfied customers.
*   **Secondary CTA:** "Learn More About Our Features"

### Features Page (`/features`)

*   **Headline:** "Unleash the Power of komoChat by Qbexel AI"
*   **Introduction:** Elaborate on the comprehensive capabilities of the AI agent.
*   **Detailed Feature Sections (each with icon/small image and description):**
    *   **Omnichannel Message Processing:** Handling messages from Facebook, Instagram, etc., across various input types (images, chat, voice).
    *   **Humanized AI Interaction:** Explain how the AI generates product queries, handles customer interactions, and maintains a human-like touch.
    *   **Automated Sales Funnel:** From initial query to order placement and fulfillment.
    *   **24/7 Customer Engagement:** Always-on availability for customer support and sales.
    *   **Data Analytics & Insights:** How the AI provides valuable data for business growth.
    *   **Seamless Integration:** Compatibility with existing social media platforms and business tools.
*   **Use Cases/Examples:** Short scenarios demonstrating the AI in action.
*   **CTA:** "See komoChat by Qbexel in Action"

### About Us Page (`/about`)

*   **Headline:** "Our Story: The Vision Behind komoChat by Qbexel"
*   **Our Mission:** "To empower social media businesses with intelligent automation that enhances customer engagement and drives sales, fostering a perfect balance between human creativity and AI efficiency."
*   **Our Vision:** "To be the leading AI solution for humanized social media business automation, revolutionizing how businesses connect with their customers."
*   **The komoChat by Qbexel Philosophy:** Elaborate on the "human and AI synergy" concept.
*   **Team Section (Optional):** If applicable, brief profiles of key team members.
*   **Values:** Innovation, Customer-Centricity, Transparency, Synergy.

### Contact Us Page (`/contact`)

*   **Headline:** "Get in Touch with komoChat by Qbexel"
*   **Contact Form:** Fields for Name, Email, Subject, Message.
*   **Contact Information:** Email address, (optional) phone number.
*   **Location (Optional):** If applicable.
*   **Social Media Links:** Icons linking to relevant social media profiles.

### Privacy Policy (`/privacy`)

*   Standard legal text outlining data collection, usage, and protection.

### Terms of Service (`/terms`)

*   Standard legal text outlining terms and conditions for using the service.

## 3. Navigation

*   **Header:** Logo, Home, Features, About Us, Contact Us.
*   **Footer:** Quick links to Home, Features, About Us, Contact Us, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service. Social media icons.

## 4. Call to Actions (CTAs)

*   **Primary CTA:** "Get Started" / "Request a Demo" (prominently displayed on Home and Features pages).
*   **Secondary CTA:** "Learn More", "Contact Sales", etc.

This content plan provides a detailed roadmap for developing the komoChat by Qbexel website, ensuring all key information is presented clearly and effectively.
