# Synapse Social Project Context

## Product Overview

Synapse Social is an enterprise-level AI agent SaaS platform designed for social media businesses. It's not just another chatbot - it's a human-like AI that feels natural and intelligent in conversations.

## Key Differentiators

- **Human-like Conversations**: Waits, listens, and responds naturally (not instant robotic replies)
- **Perfect Memory**: Remembers customer history, preferences, past orders, and conversation context
- **Multi-Modal Intelligence**: Handles text, images, and voice messages intelligently
- **Enterprise Scale**: Built to handle 100,000+ concurrent users
- **Multi-Business Support**: Perfect for agencies managing multiple clients

## Three AI Agents Architecture

1. **ChatBot Agent**: Smart context-aware RAG with conversation memory
2. **ImageBot Agent**: Visual intelligence for product recognition and matching
3. **AudioBot Agent**: Voice processing with speech-to-text and text-to-speech

## Core Philosophy

"Human creativity meets AI efficiency" - The AI enhances human potential rather than replacing it. Every interaction should feel natural, empathetic, and genuinely helpful.

## Business Model

- No free trials - focus on demos and sales contact
- Enterprise SaaS pricing (coming soon)
- Target: E-commerce brands, marketing agencies, service businesses using social media

## Technical Stack

- React with Vite
- Tailwind CSS for styling
- Lucide React for icons
- Modern responsive design principles
